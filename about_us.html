About Us

Who we are

Dr.Casa revolutionizes the way companies offer home services by providing a comprehensive and user-friendly solution for homeowners and service providers alike. Homeowners can easily browse through a diverse range of services, from plumbing and electrical work to landscaping and home cleaning, all in one convenient place. Service providers benefit from a streamlined interface that allows them to manage their schedules, communicate with clients, and showcase their expertise. With features like real-time booking, secure payment processing, and customer reviews, Dr.Casa ensures a seamless and trustworthy experience for both homeowners and service providers, fostering long-lasting relationships and empowering businesses to thrive in the digital age.

How We do it

Dr.Casa operates as a centralized hub where homeowners can easily schedule home services from a network of trusted service providers. Homeowners input their service necessity and location, triggering an algorithm that pairs them with providers based on proximity, and service expertise. Then, homeowners can view detailed profiles of the service providers, including ratings, reviews, and pricing information. Upon selecting a provider, homeowners can schedule appointments directly through the platform, with flexible options to accommodate their schedules. Service providers confirm appointments and communicate with clients. On the scheduled service date, providers arrive promptly at the designated address, equipped with the necessary tools and expertise to deliver high-quality service. Throughout the process, Dr.Casa ensures a hassle-free experience for both homeowners and service providers.
