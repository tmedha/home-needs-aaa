FAQ

Account:

How can I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking the 'Reset Password' button.

How can I edit my account information?

You can edit your account information by going to your profile settings.

If I am a service provider/customer, can I still have a customer/service provider account?

You can have both types of accounts, however the email used for one account cannot be user for the secondary account.

General:

Does Dr.Casa have a warranty?

Dr.Casa does not have a warranty.

Do I need to sign a contract?

Does Dr.Casa have any physical locations?

Dr.Casa does not have any physical locations. As of now, Dr.Casa is only a website.

Services:

What services does Dr.Casa offer?

Dr.Casa doesn't directly provide a service. Dr.Casa provides a space for companies to provide their services. All the services provided by the companies fall under one of sixteen categories.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by searching for a service. Then, clicking on the 'More Info' button of a 'Service' card. Next, clicking on your desired day and time. Finally, clicking the 'Add to Cart' button.

Can I reschedule or cancel an appointment?

Yes, you can cancel and reschedule an appointment. To reschedule an appointment, you do need to cancel your initial appointment.

Payment:

What payments methods does Dr.Casa accept?

Dr.Casa accepts credit and debit cards.

Does Dr.Casa offer gift cards?

Dr.Casa does not offer gift cards.

Can I make a purchase using financing platform like Klarna or Afterpay?

Unfortunately, currently you can only make a purchase directly on Dr.Casa's website.
